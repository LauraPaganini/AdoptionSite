Our History

This video is of our very first litter of kittens that were given to us as an agency.

For 150 years, the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals® (ASPCA) has worked tirelessly to put an end to animal abuse and neglect. We are a national leader in the areas of rescue, adoption and welfare, but none of it would be possible without ASPCA Guardians. Guardians' regular monthly gifts provide a consistent, reliable income stream that allows us to spend less time fundraising—and more time saving lives. Becoming an ASPCA Guardian is an easy, efficient and tax-deductible way to fight cruelty and make a difference for animals all year long.
